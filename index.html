About Me

My name is Spencer Purcell, and I am currently learning coding for the first time. I was born and raised in Portland, Oregon and have lived here ever since, attending Portland State University for undergrad, for which i studied Biology. However, more now I feel like switching gears and trying something a bit new, like programming, might be a great opportunity for me. I've always had an interest in computers, and that became very apparent in the middle of college for me, but I feel like I'm in the dark about a lot that goes on with websites, with databases, etc.

Just in the first few weeks of class, I've already noticed how diverse and creative you can potentially be with coding and depending on back-end versus front-end development, there is a further opportunity to diversify. Something for everyone so to speak. I was fairly apprehensive starting this program, but in short order I have come to enjoy the content of this bootcamp, now to see how far that optimism goes..
